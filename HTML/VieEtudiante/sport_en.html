SUAPS

SUAPS is the University Service of Physical and Sports Activities.

It offers more than 50 free sports activities for all levels. (list of activities)

As a DUT student, you can receive up to 5% bonus n your average each semester. The conditions of attribution are as follows :

  • Between 12 and 16 hours of attendance per semester: 1%
    Between 16 and 20 hours of attendance per semestere : 2%
    With 20 or more hours of attendance per semester : 3%
  • Level and quality of work : 1%
  • The student's personal investment : 1%
