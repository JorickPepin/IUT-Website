Program

Objectives of the degree

The objectives of the degree are :

  • to train multi-skilled technicians capable of participating in the design, realization and implementation of classic information systems and non classic (Web Information System, Geographic, Multimedia...) computer systems corresponding to the needs of the users.
  • to ensure, through the dual professional and academic aspects of the training, the possibility of immediate professional insertionlonger studies (see the page on continuing studies).

To learn more about the program, you can download the National Pedagogical Program.
