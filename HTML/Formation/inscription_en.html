Admission by right

Admission is by right for students who obtain a “good” or “very good” grade in the same year of application for the technological baccalaureate. (see decree 2008-265 of 17 March 2008)

Candidate in 2nd year

Applications for the school year 2020/2021 from 1 February 2020 to 28 May 2020.

You must go through the uB eCandidat platform and create an account to be able to apply..

Admission results will be available on 30 June 2020.

You can download the 2020 application calendar.

Recruitment Criteria

  • Matching between the Bac series and the requested training
  • Examination of grades and assessments of first and final grades including involvement and attendance, student average / class average, consideration of general culture subjects and specialty subjects related to the requested training
  • Baccalaureate tests (preparatory), Baccalaureate transcripts (holders)
  • Examination of the Avenir sheet and the opinion of the Class Council
  • Any document relating to the career path of candidates undergoing reorientation, resumption of studies, validation of prior learning, etc...
