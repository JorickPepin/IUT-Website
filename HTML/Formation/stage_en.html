Internship

Information about the internship

The internship takes place at the end of the 4th semester in a company in France or abroad (see Going abroad). It lasts a minimum of 12 weeks.

The internship is an important part of the student's training. This first contact with the reality of the profession should enable him/her to make a synthesis of the knowledge acquired during his/her training, to become aware of the socio-professional environment and to specify his/her personal aptitudes.

The subject of the internship must be identified by the company and validated by the department after consultation. The department ensures a follow-up of the internship requiring exchanges of information between the company and the department.

At the end of the placement, the student must support a placement report in front of a jury comprising, for example, the company's placement supervisor, the trainee's teacher-tutor and another teacher.
