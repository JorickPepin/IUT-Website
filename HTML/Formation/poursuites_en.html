Further studies

While the DUT prepares directly for professional integration, a very large majority of graduates of the computer science DUT continue their studies (about 80%).

Bac+3 formations

  • University degree

Whether it is in the university where your IUT depends or another one, lawsuits are possible towards the university after an DUT. You will thus enter directly in the third year and will be able to validate a licence in 1 year only.

You will then be able to continue your studies with a Master's degree in computer science (bac+5). In a master's degree, you will generally have the opportunity to specialise in a field of IT such as e-services, artificial intelligence, computer security, robotics, etc...

List of training courses in computer licensing

  • Professional degree

This year of training complements the knowledge of the DUT with a specialization. The aim of this bac+3 diploma is to give you an expertise in a specific sector of IT and to make you operational on the job market once this year of study has been completed.

List of professional computer licenses

  • DUETI

The International Diploma of Technology Studies is a diploma of level bac + 3, to be obtained after two years of DUT. It offers students the opportunity to complete their university and professional training by giving it an international dimension.

Bac+5 formations

  • Engineering school

If you have not entered an engineering school after the baccalaureate, but becoming a computer engineer is now your project, you will be able to enter an engineering school thanks to parallel admissions. Engineering courses are generally divided into two cycles: a bachelor's degree in three years and a master's degree in two years.

Some schools administer written and/or oral tests.

Some examples of computer engineering schools :

  • ENSIMAG
  • ENSIIE
  • ESIREM
  • INSA Group
  • Polytech Group
  • ...
