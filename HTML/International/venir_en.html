Learn French

At the IUT, the majority of classes are offered in French.

To improve your skills, you can take additional courses with the University of Burgundy. For more information, visit the CIEF (Centre International d'Etudes Françaises) website.

Application

As a foreign student, you have 3 possibilities to come and study :

  • As an individual :

    If you are not part of the Erasmus program and your university does not have a partnership with the IUT, please follow the instructions posted on the uB page for students coming as individuals.

  • Within the framework of Erasmus :

    If you are part of the Erasmus program, please follow the instructions on the Erasmus students page of the uB for Erasmus students.

  • As part of a partnership with your home university :

    If your university has a partnership with the IUT, please follow the instructions to this effect on the uB page for student exchanges. To check if your university is a partner with the IUT, you can consult the page Going abroad or go directly to the IUT website.
